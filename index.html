Nuwa
Tier: Meta

Stats

  • ATK: 179k
  • DEF: 9845
  • HP: 1.69m
  • SPD: 108

Description

Nuwa, the mankind creator last Nimbus Series hero with super high damage input and flexibility within the fight being able to chain together the nimbus heroes.

Nuwa's output on PvE and PvP are both extremely high being able to lash out true damage attacks, life steal a lot of damage, clear crowd control, add final damage and even ignore rebound effects and counter-attacking effects such as empress.

She lacks in survival without proper investment being the only weakness she has so far. With proper investment she can surpass ancient empress.

Recommended Talisman

  • Rapid + Fury
  • Life Steal + Combo

Recommended Spirit

  • Johan
  • Margaret

Recommended Codex

  • Thor Prayer > Mars Decree
  • Nile > King

Recommended Pet

  • SPD: Whale
  • DMG: Dragon/Bird
  • Link: Lightbringer
